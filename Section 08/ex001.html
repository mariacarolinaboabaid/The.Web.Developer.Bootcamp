Transforming Display Block In To Display Inline

And I can have a block property if we add the 'display: block' span

The inline property ignore the settings of height and width. And also, the settings of margin and padding only work horizontally.

On the contrary, the inline property respects all of this settings.

An between, there is the choice of inline-block property, that works like an inline display, but respect the settings.
